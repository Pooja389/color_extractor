GUIDE PAGE

Learn how to upload an image and get colors out of it

How to Upload an Image

Follow these simple steps to upload an image:

  1. On the Home page, locate the image upload section.
  2. Click on the Choose File button to open your file explorer.
  3. Select an image file from your device. Supported formats are PNG, JPG, and JPEG.
  4. After selecting the file, click on the Upload button to upload the image.

Supported File Formats

Make sure your image is in one of the following formats:

  • PNG (.png)
  • JPG (.jpg)
  • JPEG (.jpeg)

Once uploaded, the application will analyze the image and display the top colors extracted from it on the Home page.

Additional Tips

If your image isn't displaying correctly after upload, make sure:

  • The file size is within the acceptable range for uploads.
  • The filename contains no special characters or spaces.
